PMMA Lens

Since 1949 Intraocular lenses have been made of PMMA, due to its lightness, clarity and stability. Even today, PMMA is the material of choice. PMMA offers outstanding stability and optical quality for a predictable outcome and maximum performance. Millions of odd eyes have been implanted with our basic and premium range of PMMA lenses with excellent outcome. Thanks to the inherent quality of the material.
